Conclusion & Summary

After evaluating four different classification models, we can draw the following conclusions:

  • The KNN model achieved the highest final test accuracy at 97.89%, making it the top-performing model in this comparison.
  • The Random Forest model also showed very strong performance with a high cross-validation score (97.52%) and test accuracy (97.34%).
  • All models demonstrated perfect precision and recall for the 'Mixture' and 'Smoke' classes, indicating they can identify these gases with very high confidence.
  • The LSTM model, while still effective, had the lowest accuracy at 95.70%, with noticeably lower precision and recall for the 'NoGas' and 'Perfume' classes compared to other models.
  • Overall, for this specific gas detection task, traditional machine learning models like KNN and Random Forest slightly outperformed the deep learning (LSTM) approach.
